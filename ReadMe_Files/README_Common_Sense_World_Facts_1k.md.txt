Dataset Overview
The dataset provides extensive coverage across multiple domains of common sense and world knowledge:

Core Categories (1,000 problems total):

Fire safety, gas leaks, natural disasters

First aid basics, emergency procedures

Home safety, electrical safety

Traffic safety, personal security

Social norms and etiquette

Communication skills and relationships

Cultural awareness and diversity

Language and social interaction

Human body facts and basic anatomy

Nutrition, exercise, and wellness

Food safety and hygiene

Mental health and sleep importance

Geography & World Knowledge (~434 problems)

Countries, capitals, and landmarks

Physical geography (mountains, rivers, oceans)

Climate, weather patterns, and seasons

International awareness and global citizenship

Work & Career Skills (~448 problems)

Professional behavior and workplace etiquette

Transportation & Travel (~131 problems)

Insurance and financial planning

Key Features:

1,000 exactly formatted rows with instruction, input, output structure

Question variety: "What" (711), "Why" (225), "How" (37), others (27)

Practical focus on knowledge needed for daily life

Global perspective covering different cultures and regions

Safety emphasis on protecting health and well-being

Life skills integration combining knowledge with application

Educational Value:

Develops practical life skills and safety awareness

Builds cultural competency and social understanding

Provides foundational knowledge for informed citizenship

Teaches essential skills for personal and professional success

Enhances decision-making abilities in everyday situations

Promotes global awareness and environmental consciousness

The dataset emphasizes actionable knowledge that people need to navigate modern life effectively, from basic safety procedures to understanding global systems. It serves as a comprehensive foundation for common sense reasoning and practical wisdom.
