## 🧮 SAMPLE ENHANCED CONTENT

### **Original Sample (274 chars average)**:
```
Instruction: Calculate: 15 + 27
Output: To add 15 + 27: 15 + 27 = 42
```

### **Enhanced Sample (627 chars average)**:
```
Instruction: Calculate and explain the relationship: 15 + 27
Output: Mathematical Analysis:
Operation: Addition (combining quantities)
Addends: 15 and 27
Process: 15 + 27 = 42

Concept Relationships:
• Addition is the inverse of subtraction: 42 - 27 = 15
• Commutative property: 15 + 27 = 27 + 15 = 42
• This represents combining 15 units with 27 units
• In number line terms: starting at 15, move 27 positions right
• Decomposition: 15 + 27 = (10+5) + (20+7) = 30 + 12 = 42
• Pattern: Adding 27 to any number increases it by exactly 27 units
```
