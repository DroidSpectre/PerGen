Understanding and recognizing your own emotions

Identifying emotional triggers and patterns

Emotional vocabulary and granularity

Self-reflection and mindfulness practices

Distinguishing thoughts from feelings

Self-Regulation (~598 problems)

Managing emotions effectively (anger, anxiety, stress)

Impulse control and delayed gratification

Stress management and coping strategies

Emotional flexibility and adaptability

Bouncing back from setbacks and disappointments

Empathy (~515 problems)

Understanding others' emotions and perspectives

Reading nonverbal emotional cues

Perspective-taking and compassionate responding

Supporting others through difficult times

Managing emotional contagion and boundaries

Social Skills (~521 problems)

Building and maintaining relationships

Communication and conflict resolution

Group dynamics and team collaboration

Leadership and influence through EI

Professional relationship management

Advanced Topics:

Neuroscience of Emotions - Brain science behind EI

Cultural Emotional Intelligence - EI across cultures

Complex EI Concepts - Advanced theories and applications

Key Features:

1,000 exactly formatted rows with instruction, input, output structure

Question variety: "What" (593), "How" (407)

Progressive difficulty from basic concepts to advanced applications

Practical scenarios with real-world applications

Evidence-based content drawing from psychology and neuroscience research

Comprehensive coverage of all EI domains and skill areas

Educational Value:

Develops comprehensive emotional intelligence competency

Builds self-awareness and emotional regulation skills

Enhances empathy and social connection abilities

Improves stress management and resilience

Strengthens relationship and communication skills

Provides practical tools for personal and professional growth

Supports mental health and wellbeing

Teaches emotional skills for leadership and influence

Offers strategies for supporting others emotionally

Builds cultural competency in emotional interactions

The dataset emphasizes practical, actionable emotional intelligence skills that people need to thrive in personal relationships, professional environments, and overall life satisfaction. It covers everything from basic emotion recognition to advanced emotional leadership and cultural competency.
