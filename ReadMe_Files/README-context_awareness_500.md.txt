# Context Awareness Dataset (500 samples)

## Dataset Overview
This dataset provides comprehensive training for situational awareness, contextual understanding, and adaptive response generation. It combines world knowledge with situational intelligence to develop context-sensitive AI behavior across diverse scenarios.

## Core Context Areas (500 problems total):

### Social Context & Etiquette (~63 problems)
- Conversation interruption appropriateness
- Setting influence on topic selection
- Formal vs. informal name usage
- Privacy cue recognition
- Group dynamic awareness

### Safety & Emergency Context (~63 problems)
- Emergency service communication
- Environmental safety factor assessment
- Situational danger recognition
- Weather-related safety adaptation
- Risk assessment and prevention

### Professional Context (~63 problems)
- Company culture communication adaptation
- Meeting formality level determination
- Industry-specific behavioral expectations
- Management escalation protocols
- Client-specific presentation approaches

### Cultural Context (~63 problems)
- Gift-giving cultural appropriateness
- Business meeting cultural protocols
- Religious dietary considerations
- Personal space cultural variations
- Age-related cultural expectations

### Temporal Context (~62 problems)
- Time-appropriate communication
- Historical context influence on attitudes
- Seasonal business planning considerations
- Topic timing appropriateness
- Generational communication preferences

### Geographic Context (~62 problems)
- Regional communication variations
- Geographic emergency preparedness
- Urban vs. rural lifestyle influences
- Climate-based daily planning
- International business considerations

### Technological Context (~62 problems)
- Platform-specific communication styles
- Security context considerations
- Device-influenced design decisions
- Privacy setting requirements
- Connectivity impact on work arrangements

### Legal & Ethical Context (~62 problems)
- Situational legal obligation determination
- Ethical decision-making in gray areas
- Workplace legal behavior considerations
- Privacy context for information sharing
- Professional regulatory compliance

## Dataset Characteristics

**Format**: Instruction-Input-Output structure
- **Instruction**: Context-dependent scenario or question
- **Input**: Empty (self-contained contextual problems)
- **Output**: Multi-factor analysis with contextual considerations

**Educational Focus**:
- Multi-dimensional context analysis
- Adaptive response strategies
- Cultural sensitivity development
- Situational appropriateness assessment
- Environmental factor consideration
