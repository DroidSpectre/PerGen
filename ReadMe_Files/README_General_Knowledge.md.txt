Dataset Overview
The general knowledge dataset provides broad coverage across many subject areas that constitute basic education and cultural literacy:

Core Knowledge Areas:

Geography (~61 problems) - Countries, capitals, landmarks, physical features, currencies

History (~89 problems) - Major events, dates, historical figures, wars, discoveries

Science (~101 problems) - Basic concepts from physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy

Animals & Nature (~95 problems) - Species facts, characteristics, habitats, classifications

Arts & Culture (~59 problems) - Famous works, artists, music, languages, traditions

Human Body & Health (~72 problems) - Anatomy, physiology, basic medical knowledge

Mathematics (~24 problems) - Basic concepts, geometry, numbers, calculations

Sports & Games (~28 problems) - Rules, records, famous events, Olympic facts

Technology (~12 problems) - Inventions, modern devices, internet basics

Additional Coverage:

Space & Astronomy - Planets, solar system, space exploration

Food & Cooking - Cuisines, ingredients, nutrition, cultural dishes

Languages & Communication - Basic phrases, language facts, writing systems

Religion & Philosophy - World religions, basic concepts, famous thinkers

Economics - Currencies, basic financial concepts, economic terms

Miscellaneous Facts - Everyday knowledge, common sense, practical information

Key Features:

500 exactly formatted rows with instruction, input, output structure

Varied question types: "What" questions (318), "How" questions (92), "Which" questions (49)

Multiple difficulty levels from basic facts to more complex explanations

Factually accurate information suitable for educational purposes

Global perspective covering different cultures and regions

Educational Value:

Develops broad cultural literacy and foundational knowledge

Covers topics typically found in general education curricula

Includes both memorizable facts and conceptual understanding

Balances accessibility with educational depth

Suitable for trivia, educational games, and knowledge assessment

The dataset emphasizes basic to intermediate difficulty (338 basic, 36 intermediate, 17 advanced problems), making it accessible while still educational. All facts are presented accurately with helpful context and explanations that go beyond simple answers to provide educational value.
